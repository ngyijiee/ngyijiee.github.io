Pickupp

Pickupp is a delivery and e-commerce platform operating in Singapore, Hong Kong, Malaysia and Taiwan.

Delivery Agent App

The Task: Identify the current problems and dictate the product direction for the Delivery Agent App.

Objective: Optimise the existing design for the agents to efficiently view and pick jobs at a glance, deliver orders, and plan trips.

Scope: 6 weeks

Category: UX Research, UX Design, UI Design

Role: Sole designer. Conducted user interviews, created wireframes and prototypes.

Team: Product Manager, Software Engineers, Operations Team.

Design Solution

I restructured information, primarily to communicate Location, Time, Job Earnings. And for secondary but crucial information, those can be communicated through icons for faster understanding. Agents are looking at the screen for long hours, hence further research was done in order to create a new Accessible Color Palette

What's Next

I will validate all design decisions based on performance of key metrics such as active users, number of events and retention rates.

What I Learned

The biggest lesson I learned at this startup is the tradeoff between quality and cost. Nothing is ever 'done' as there's always room for improvement. However, designers have to learn to balance between time, cost and quality constraints.

Redesigning the Pickupp Delivery Agent mobile app has been an incredible experience, and I'm really grateful that I got to work on many platforms, and that Pickupp offered me projects with big impact that I would have ownership of.
