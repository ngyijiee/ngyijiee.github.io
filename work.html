HomeChef

A meal-prep and delivery service for home cook food.

The Inconvenience of home cooked food.

An emerging user need based on interviewing a diverse group of food enthusiasts, is that it is challenging to find time to cook on weekdays. As an exploratory research, I conducted several contextual inquiries & semi-structured interviews to confirm the problem and learn about their pain-points. Interview analysis - It was apparent that the majority were cooking 3 times a week on average and people who loved to cook had a need to sell their food on a virtual platform where they can operate without a brick and mortar store in cities where rent was higher.
